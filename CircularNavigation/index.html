Covid-19 Sentiment Analysis

The Sentiments of Indian people related to lockdown and COVID-19 during the different phases of lockdown is shown with the help of Line and Bar graph below. Here four different Line graphs are shown representing the 4 different phases of lockdown which started from 25/March/2020 and upto 31/May/2020. After that one Bar graph is shown which represents complete sentiments of all the 4 phases together. The First Phase was from :- 25/March/2020 - 14/April/2020 The Second Phase was from :- 15/March/2020 - 03/May/2020 The third Phase was from :- 04/May/2020 - 17/May/2020 The Fourth Phase was from :- 18/May/2020 - 31/May/2020
